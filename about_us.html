Our brief History

Greenwood Library was established in 1990 with the aim of providing a comprehensive collection of books and resources to the community. Over the years, we have expanded our services to include digital resources, community events, and educational programs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote literacy and lifelong learning by providing access to a wide range of information resources and services. We strive to create a welcoming environment for all members of the community.

Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated librarians and staff who are passionate about helping our patrons find the information they need. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and fostering a love of reading.
